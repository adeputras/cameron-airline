SALES EXECUTIVE & PARTNER

An award-winning agent and Partner of N G Farah, Cameron Airlie consistently ranks as one of the Eastern Suburbs top performing and most in-demand real estate agents. Widely respected amongst his colleagues and clients alike, Cameron is known for his approachable, easy going personality, exceptional dedication and vast knowledge of the South Eastern coastal region.

With over 17 years of industry experience, Cameron is credited with numerous reserve breaking sales and an impressive clearance rate. He has received many accolades for his dedication including being ranked in the prestigious 2017 REB Top 100 Agents in Australia and holding the current title for N G Farah’s No. 1 Sales Agent. Always up for a challenge, Cameron was the agent of choice for contestants Josh and Jenna on The Block All Stars 2013 which saw him gain national recognition.

Together with his dedicated team of Sales Associates, Cameron always goes beyond expectations to ensure rewarding results for all involved. A truly rare breed of agent, his transparent approach and exceptional commitment to every client make him an unquestionable choice for both vendors and buyers.

As a lifetime local and advocate of the Eastern Suburbs lifestyle, Cameron has strong community ties sponsoring various local sporting clubs and charity events. Out of the trademark socks he can be found hitting the surf, getting in a round of golf or barracking for the red and green.
