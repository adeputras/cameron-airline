8 Byrne Avenue, South Coogee

Prized South Coogee Residence on 556.4sqm, Park Pocket

  • 5
  • 3
  • 4

In a premier parkside avenue, this palatial residence makes an ideal home for the large family. A striking solid brick and concrete construction, it has high ceilings and district views. Outside a lush poolside barbeque area makes the ultimate highlight for the family who like to entertain. Set between Coogee and Maroubra Beaches, it's a short stroll to Latham Park, South Coogee Public School, the coastal walk and rock pools. It's also located within close proximity to the popular International French School of Sydney and Mount Sinai College.


  • Multi-level living spaces include library/home office
  • Wide alfresco area features resort style solar heated saltwater pool
  • Open plan granite kitchen features Miele appliances
  • Upstairs living wrapped in windows offers district views
  • Large bedrooms, two open to balconies
  • Infrared sauna, spa bathroom, master with ensuite
  • Rare dual driveway access plus tandem garage parking
